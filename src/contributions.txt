Controls

Up/Down/Left/Right arrow keys = LEM movement
Shift/Space = Down/Up LEM movement
Q/E = Rotate left/right LEM movement

1 = LZ1 cam
2 = LZ2 cam
3 = LZ3 cam
4 = Lander onboard cam
5 = Top-down Lander cam
6 = Below Lander cam

` = Toggle tracking cam
R = EasyCam wide view
T = EasyCam wide view, lander position


U = Restart on landing/death
Z = Toggle Altitude sensor
A = Toggle Fuel/Altitude HUD
